NMM-Family Diwali Dhamaka 2017

नमस्कार मंडळी,

As you all are aware that, very soon we will be approaching to DIWALI, a festival of lights, sweets, togetherness and unity. Our festivals are one of the modes that bring all of us together from our routinely busy lives. It is our pleasure to invite you to the NMM Diwali celebrations-2017, to be held on October 21st, 2017 at the Hindu Temple Social Hall. This year NMM is introducing new theme for the Diwali celebration. Families/Group of friends or kids can be considered as individual performance (1-2 min.) to support this theme. There are various categories of perfromances like group dance, singing, jugalbandi and skit. Spots are limited for all these categories. Also, this year NMM endeavor to expand its horizon to make this platform available to wide audience/participants from other communities as well.
