GANPATI BAPPA MORYA!!!

गणेश विसर्जन (Immersion of Lord Ganesh idol)

नमस्कार मंडळी,

We are excited to let you know, this year Hindu Temple of Omaha is organizing Ganesh Visarjan (विसर्जन) with Nebraska Marathi Mandal’s (NMM) Initiative. This is one more opportunity for us to care about Mother Earth with Ganapati worship. Thanks to Tulsi Indian Groceries 402-330-8575 and Patel Grocers 402-905-9999 for their commitment to sale biodegradable Ganesh Idols. We will send additional details of Visarjan timing soon. Requesting your cooperation in these Go Green efforts, we all can follow little steps to take care of environment and to make it easy we have following guide lines.

  • Ganapati Idol should be less than 1 feet (12 inches) height.

  • The idol material (including colors) should be complete biodegradable.

  • Please bring your Idol purchase receipt with your name on receipt.

  • Please avoid flowers as visarjan space is limited. Please put them in your yard.
