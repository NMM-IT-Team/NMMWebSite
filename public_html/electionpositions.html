Benefit

As a Part of our election pilot project 2018 NMM has decided to give free event entry to board members and their immediate family members. Each board member will have choice to participate or opt out this benefit.

Eligibility

Candidate for the office shall be a member in good standing and must be a member for at list six months prior to this date.

For Executive Board position one should have one year NMM board experience.

Candidate should be in good standing positions with the state of Nebraska.

Needs to be in good standing with NMM, by paying their annual membership dues

Member shall follow and honor the NMM board and the guidelines in the event of dishonoring NMM board the respective members may be subject to expulsion.

Expectations

It is highly important that the person should understand- This is an opportunity to serve for community a noble work and must serve/work with integrity and respect.

Willingness to work as a volunteer, it is expected to work along with other NMM volunteers with honor for the success of NMM.

Should be respectful to NMM board and NMM members and receptive to suggestions.

Should be able to attend meetings and participate in all the discussions and time to time willingly carry over the entire event as a coordinator.

Duties are not limited to the position description only; should be willingly able to participate into all NMM activities and event and should be willing to take additional task other than position responsibilities, duties to help other board members.

Apart from their duties, member(s) should extend his/her efforts to manage other important tasks, if require especially during an auspicious event(s) (ie. Ganeshotsava, Diwali)

NMM CODE OF CONDUCT

Board members shall work within the framework of the By-Laws.

Board members shall attend all board meetings. Any director or officer, who without valid reason, fails to attend two consecutive meetings or fails to perform his/her duties shall be subject to expulsion at the discretion of the Executive Committee.

No board member shall use his/her membership of the Mandal for personal profit or gain. Anyone found guilty of this is liable to be expelled from his/her office or membership of the Mandal by action of the Executive Committee.

No board members shall use NMM’s name to create email, groups without NMMs permission.

Board members shall treat each other with respect and shall conduct business in a professional manner. Any director or officer, who has been reported for misconduct on more than one occasion shall be subject to expulsion at the discretion of the Executive Committee.

President

To function as the head of the organization

To preside over the general body meetings

To lead the organization with conviction and fairness to promote the wellbeing of its members

To serve as an ex-officio member of all committees created for effective functioning of the organization

To serve as a liaison with the other associations in the state and at national level (BMM) and working with them to keep lines of communication with Maharashtrians open for members of the organization

All decisions concerning the organization; taken by the President, shall only be taken after consultation with the elected board members AND/OR after obtaining a majority vote from the board of directors.

Secretary

Keeping records of the organization’s activities

Informing the board members about meetings

Informing the members about dates, times and agendas of general body meetings

Keeping and distributing, as appropriate, minutes of all meetings

Preparing and submitting an annual report of the organization’s activities

Treasurer

To keep the records of monies received and expenditures incurred

To present a financial report to the board members as needed

To present an annual financial report at the annual general body meeting

To deposit funds in a plan decided by the board

To raise funds for NMM and other causes with fundraising ideas.

Joint Secretary

To work with the Secretary to share workload

To performs the duties of the secretary in the absence of the secretary

Joint Treasurer

To work with the treasurer to share workload

To perform the duties of the treasurer in the absence of the treasurer

Member Relations

To handle public relations with members who've paid their dues

To encourage new members to join the Mandal

To present member related issues and concerns in front of the board

To gather feedback on an ongoing basis from the members and encourage participation

Digital Media Coverage

To manage NMM posts related to event announcements, greetings, etc. on social media platforms like facebook

To manage email communication regarding event announcements, greetings, sharing post event pics, videos, etc.

To welcome new group members on social media sites such as facebook, twitter, etc.

To post NMM events on IAN and UNO social media pages such as facebook, desiomaha.com, etc.

To welcome new group members who join NMM group on facebook.

Working with board members to gather event details, creating event flyer and posting into Hindu temple, Indian grocery stores etc

Event Decoration

Responsible for decorating the venue for each event as per the theme of the event.

Responsible for decoration plan and budget and should be presenting to board well in advance.

Responsible for all decoration including temple, IAN event to present NMM.

Can work with other team members within or outside NMM board for help with decoration.

Cultural Coordinator I

Possesses information and knowledge of the Maharashtra culture, teachings, and traits to bring values to the community with action and approaches.

Arrange all cultural related activities to present Maharashtra cultural in NMM-Other events.

Programs such as dance, Dramas, Skit and musical performances mostly conducted and present in Marathi language and related to Maharashtrian culture, history, religion and etiquettes

Arrange event and celebration specific activities other than drama, songs and dance and skits.

Gather ideas from members and Board members and to implement those in NMM event for kids and adults.

Present Maharashtra culture and teach cultural values through event activities to Kids and community members.

Games and Entertainment

To maintain a set of games and entertainment ideas for each event

To work with the event coordinators during each event for proposing and executing creative games and entertainment options

Yearly Planning Committee

To prepare the yearly plan with events, dates, and event coordinators for the rest of this year and at the beginning of next year.

To create google calendar entries and send invitations to board members

To coordinate with event heads and food leads for each event on their activities

To ensure that the event and food leads are properly leveraging internal or external resources for executing their events and help them with pulling in additional resources if needed

To ensure that the planned events, ideally, do not clash with another major event
