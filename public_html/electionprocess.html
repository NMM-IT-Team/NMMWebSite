We are looking for enthusiastic, passionate individuals to be proud members of NMM, who would like to make a difference in the community by supporting initiatives such as GO GREEN and be a part of NMM’s decision making. The 2017 board took a democratic approach to put together a pilot project in order to elect board members, and would like to continue with this program. Current board members will be a part of the election committee and will monitor the process.


NMM Election Timelines

Nomination deadline – 12/10/2018
Nominate yourself by sending an email to: nebraskamarathimandal@gmail.com. You must nominate yourself to be considered.

Application confirmation on 12/13/2018
All self-nominated nominees will receive an email communication about potential candidates for the position to which they have applied, after the nomination entry window is closed.

Application reconsider deadline 12/17/2018
If anyone would like to reconsider your self-nomination at this step please inform NMM by emailing at nebraskamarathimandal@gmail.com.

Voting start 12/20/2018
A survey will be created for each position and sent to all NMM members for voting. Based on survey results the NMM position will be filled. The results will be sent in an email to those who applied. All 2018 board members will be introduced at an upcoming NMM event and over social media.

Voting ends 12/23/2018
All 2019 board members will be contacted by NMM communication, followed by an announcement. 2018 board will meet with 2019 board for proper handover, public introduction will be held in NMM Tilgul (तिळगुळ) program on January 13, 2019 tentatively.

Jan 14, 2019 board officially takes charge of NMM
